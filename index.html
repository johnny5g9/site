FAQ

Common questions before booking

How can we improve photo quality on game day?

Bench access helps a lot. It improves shooting angles, reaction shots, and overall image quality throughout the game.

How fast is delivery?

Single-game galleries are typically delivered within 48 hours, and multi-game tournament coverage is prioritized for fast turnaround.

How many photos do we receive?

Single Game coverage includes 60+ edited photos. Tournament Package coverage includes 180+ edited photos for 3+ games.

How far do you travel in the Triangle?

I regularly cover Raleigh, Cary, Wake Forest, and Garner rinks, with nearby Triangle travel available depending on schedule.

Do you offer team + individual photo requests?

Yes. I can prioritize specific players, lines, or moments you request in advance.

What are your rates and deliverables?

Single Game coverage is $75 per game with 60+ edited photos. Tournament Package coverage is $65 per game for 3+ games with 180+ edited photos.

How do we book?

Send an Instagram DM or complete the booking form on this page with your date, rink location, and team details. Typical response time is within 24 hours.
